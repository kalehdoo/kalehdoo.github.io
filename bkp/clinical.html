Clinical Trials Approaches

Industry moving from Traditional (Physical) method to Virtual (Mobile) method with New Technology adoption

Traditional Clinical Trials Method

  • Low Patient Participation
  • Less Patient centric approach
  • Long lead time to complete study
  • High cost structure

Virtual Clinical Trials Method

  • Ease in Patient identification & recruitment
  • Decentralized approach
  • New Technology Adoption
  • Improve patient safety
  • Cost effective & efficient for rare diseases
