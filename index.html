Kalehdoo

Join our mission to support school education and skill development initiatives.

School Education

Primary school education is the foundation that enables students support themselves in their career. We support initiatives to help children get better education and build a stronger foundation that helps create an ecosystem critical to local innovation and development.

Skill Development

Supports initiatives providing skill-based training and development required to empower local people with the required knowledge and expertise to establish their own micro-businesses.

Software Development

Provide software development and consulting services to small businesses considering their unique needs. We also provide training and career counseling to students who want to become software professionals.

Micro-business Consulting

Micro-businesses are critical to empower communities to be self-reliant and sustainable in the longer run while bringing growth to the local economy. We provide business consulting and operational support services in setting up new micro-business or to help grow an existing business.
