Clinical Research Industry Challenges

  • Low Patient Participation
    • Low recruitment rates (3%) of eligible patients
    • 40% drop-out rate of patients involved in phase III studies
    • Barriers to participation due to travel distances to reach research centers
    • Geographically dispersed groups or rare disease populations
  • Less Patient centric approach
    • Ease of participation for patients
    • Severe events actions/reporting
  • Long lead time to complete study
  • High cost structure
