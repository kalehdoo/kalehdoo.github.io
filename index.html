Kalehdoo

A mission empowering local communities to become more self-reliant through education, entrepreneurship, and preventive healthcare.

Education

School education is the foundation that enables students to support themselves in their career. We support initiatives to help children get quality education empowering them with the required skills and knowledge to become a better person in life.

Social Entrepreneurship

Supports initiatives providing skill-based training required to empower local people with the required knowledge and expertise to establish their own micro-businesses. To develop an ecosystem of Social Entrepreneurship by promoting and implementing novel applications solving socio-economic and environmental problems of the communities in rural areas.

Cultural and Health Awareness

Support initiatives to increase cultural and Health awareness to increase healthy life-span. Spead awareness about the benefits of adopting healthy life-style, like healthy food and yoga, preventing health problems and avoiding healthcare costs.
