Founder

Manohar Rana

Manohar is passionate about designing and building Data analytics, Business Intelligence and Data Warehouse (BIDW) solutions. Currently working as a business intelligence and data warehouse consultant at PRA Health Sciences in Raleigh. Previously, Manohar has worked with big and small companies like Oracle, Deloitte, Icon Resources and few others in consulting roles for over 15 years. He has a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Pune and is currently pursuing Masters in Business Administration at Poole College of Management at North Carolina State University (NCSU) with concentration in Bio-sciences Management and Analytics. He supports primary education and skill development initiatives through his mission Kalehdoo.
