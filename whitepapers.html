Transforming Clinical Studies through Technologies

Published On: 14-Sep-2020

COVID-19 has fundamentally changed the world and having an impact on every aspect of our life. Besides day to day activities, industrial practices and technologies are in for significant changes. The area of clinical studies is no exception. The efforts on virtualization though had already started in this industry are getting accelerated in the CoVID-19 ravaged world. Advances in communication, information technology and sensor technologies are key enablers for greater virtualization. Three primary technologies that are bound to transform the industry are: Multidimensional Data Analysis, Blockchain, and AI/ML.
