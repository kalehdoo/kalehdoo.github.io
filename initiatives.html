Our Initiatives

Scholarship Program

Kalehdoo has started BIDSANTO scholarship program to provide financial assistance to deserving high school students. A campaign has started encouraging people to start scholarships in the names of their family members. We have initiated the program with Gramin Sewa Ashram, a non-profit organisation working at the grass root level to improve the socio-economic conditions in the rural areas, to identify and recommend the deserving students. Kalehdoo would extend this program to other similar organisations in future.

Started in April 2019, Kalehdoo has provided 3 scholarships of INR 10,000 each under BIDSANTO scholarship program.

Digital Library Program

We understand the importance of digital technology for education. We have launched an initiave under KALEHDOO COMMUNITY DIGITAL LIBRARY to set up a Community digital library inside the office of Gramin Sewa Ashram. The library would be equipped with laptops and other devices encouraging students to use the facility to learn and develop new skills. We strongly believe that the access to digital information will drastically enhance the knowledge of the students helping them in leading a successful career. We have started a campaign encouraging people to donate new/used devices for the digital library. In future, we would setup many such libraries.

So far we have been successful in recieving 1 laptop for the digital library. The Digital Infrastructure would help us in launching an ambitious initiative to encourage Reading at early stages of school children.
