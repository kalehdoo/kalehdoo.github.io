Recommended learning resources

  • Reading Comprehension
    ReadTheory is a wonderful reading comprehension resource for students. It has a vast library of reading passages that are informative and safe for children. ReadTheory's adaptive approach automatically adjusts the reading level as you continue reading. Students and teachers can track their reading history and lexile score. This program is completely free.
  • Vocabulary
    Vocabulary is an excellent resource to improve vocabulary in an intelligent way. Vocabulary.com helps to master new words using adaptive learning games. Students can create new word lists and practice them. There is a special section for mastering spellings. This website version is free.
  • Science Technology Engineering Math (STEM)
    Khanacademy is by far the best resource for learning Math, Science, computer programming, history, economics and many more subjects for students from kindergarten to college level. Khan Academy offers practice exercises, instructional videos, and a personalized learning dashboard that empower learners to study at their own pace in and outside of the classroom. The content is well organised and easy to understand. This program is completely free.
