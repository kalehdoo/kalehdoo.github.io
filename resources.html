Recommended learning resources

Reading Comprehension

ReadTheory is a wonderful resource for reading. It has a vast library of reading comprehension content. ReadTheory's adaptive approach fosters improvement by automatically meeting learners at their own, individual ability levels. Signup takes seconds and no time is spent vetting assignments. Progress is shown on an intuitive report, replete with actionable, meaningful insights. This program is completely free.

Digital Library Program

Kalehdoo understands the importance of digital technology for education. As a pilot project, we are in the process of setting up a digital library inside the office of Gramin Sewa Ashram. The library would be equipped with laptops and other devices encouraging students to use the facility to learn and develop new skills. We strongly believe that the access to digital information will drastically enhance the knowledge of the students helping them in leading a successful career. We will start a campaign to encourage people to donate new/used devices for the digital library. In future, we would setup many such libraries.
